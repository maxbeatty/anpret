GeoLorean

GeoLorean showed your friends’ check-ins from pop­u­lar loca­tion-based social net­works like Gowalla, Brightkite, and foursquare in one stream.

Each loca­tion ser­vice had their own unique appeal which is why peo­ple used more than one. Some­times you sim­ply wanted to know where your friends last checked in regard­less of what ser­vice they used. Other times, you wanted to check in on all of your ser­vices so we included check.in as well.

We were featured in the App Store as New and Noteworthy.
