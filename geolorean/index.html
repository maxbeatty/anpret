GeoLorean is a simple service that shows your friends' check-ins from popular location based social networks like Gowalla, Brightkite, and Foursquare in one stream.

Each location service has their own unique appeal which is why people use more than one. Sometimes you simply want to know where your friends last checked in regardless of what service they used. Other times, you may want to check in on all of your services so we've included check.in as well.
